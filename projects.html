Projects

As a person who loves learning about almost everything, I've done quite a few projects throughout the years. I'm still figuring out my path in life, and these activities help me discover what I like and don't like.

At the moment, I'm a Product Manager Intern on the Internal Technology Products team at Emerson Collective. At Rice, I'm a Full Stack Developer at RiceApps, a student-run organization that creates web apps benefitting the Rice Community. I'm also a Data Analytics Consultant through 180 Degrees Consulting and a barista at Audrey's, our on campus specialty coffee shop!

I also do a variety of STEM projects at the intersection of tech and business. I'm currently the website manager at Bcauseican. I've been a Full Stack Project Lead at STEM-Away where I developed open source code for a forum platform. I've also worked on UIowa Research on business analytics and a personal research project about the role of Websites in Businesses.

Back in high school, I prided myself in my involvement in the community. I dedicated over 900 hours serving my local community through Cool Science, a STEM education program, and Get Involved Foundation, a non-profit engaging local youth in social impact. I was also a leader of various high school clubs including Girls Tech, Business, and TSA TEAMS.
