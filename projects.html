Projects

As a person who loves learning about almost everything, I've done quite a few projects throughout the years. In fact, I've taken on endeavors ranging from encouraging volunteering to crowdfunding research to volleyball. I'm still figuring out my path in life, and these activities help me discover what I like and don't like. Without further ado, read on!

Volunteering: Community service was my thing in high school (as in 900+ hours of dedication). I started out trying all sorts of gigs like cooking at homeless shelters or supervising toddlers at the YMCA or translating school newsletters into Spanish. In the end, I do have two most meaningful projects: Cool Science and Get Involved Foundation.

STEM Projects: I'm particularly interested in the intersection of tech and business. I'm currently the webmaster at Bcauseican. I've also explored this by working on a UIowa Research Project where I helped quantify the efficacy of crowdfunding projects. I've also worked on personal research about the role of Websites in Businesses as well as open source code for Discourse through STEM-Away.

High School Clubs: Involvement in student organizations was a great way for me to check out my many avenues of interest. These clubs include Girls Tech, Business, and TSA TEAMS. I'm definitely looking to get involved in more clubs in college!
