Student Organizations

Girls Tech Club (Co-President): Our mission was to create a community for women to explore and STEM to narrow the gender gap in the tech industry. As Co-President, I organized interactive tech lessons, speaker events, and outreach events.

Business (VP of DECA): Gunn Business is a competition team that prepares students to be academically prepared, community oriented, professionally responsible, and experienced in leadership through involvement in the National DECA and FBLA organizations. In addition to competitions, I helped implement the mentor system and keep communications up to date.

TSA TEAMS (President): The Research Science Innovation Club engages high school students in engineering challenges, research talks, and math/science lessons to further interest in STEM. We also prepare members to compete in the TSA Tests of Engineering Aptitude, Mathematics, and Science (TEAMS) engineering competition.
