UIowa Business Analytics Research

As part of the Secondary Student Training Program (SSTP), I worked on business analytics research with faculty at the University of Iowa. Our project used machine learning models to quantify the efficacy of crowdfunding projects on Kickstarter. The ultimate goal was to aid entrepreneurs in making more successful campaigns.
